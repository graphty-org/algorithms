Eigenvector Centrality Explained

Eigenvector centrality measures influence based on the quality of connections, not just quantity.

It's like measuring popularity - being connected to popular people makes you more popular too!

Key Concept:

Eigenvector centrality identifies influential nodes by considering both:

  • Direct connections: Who you're connected to
  • Quality of connections: How influential your connections are
  • Recursive calculation: Influence flows through the network

Unlike degree centrality, a node with few but highly influential connections can have high eigenvector centrality. It's the principle behind Google's PageRank!
